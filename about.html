Our History & Journey

From humble beginnings to a global fire movement

2010

The Birth of a Vision

Pastor Bob Ashley received the divine mandate to start a ministry that would ignite passion for God and transform lives through the fire of the Holy Spirit.

2012

First Gatherings

Started with home fellowship meetings in Koforidua, Ghana. The "Fire Generation" movement began with just 12 committed believers.

2015

Rhema Fire Christian Church Established

Officially registered as a church with a clear vision: "To raise a generation on fire for God, impacting nations through the Gospel."

2018

FiredSpirit City Acquisition

Purchased and dedicated 5 acres of land in Koforidua as the permanent headquarters, named "FiredSpirit City - The Prayer Capital."

2020

Digital Expansion

Launched online streaming, reaching a global audience during the pandemic. Started FirePod for audio messages.

2023

Multi-National Impact

Established branches in 3 countries. Launched Help Orphan Project and SheGold Impact community initiatives.

2026

Current Vision

Focus on the 2026 theme: "GRATITUDE" (Eucharistia). Building permanent structures and expanding ministry schools.

Our Vision

To raise a generation on fire for God, impacting nations through the Gospel, and establishing God's kingdom on earth through passionate worship, radical discipleship, and transformative community impact.

Our Mission

To ignite passion for God in every heart, transform lives through the fire of the Holy Spirit, equip believers for kingdom service, and demonstrate God's love through compassionate outreach to communities.

Our Core Values

  • Passion for God
  • Compassion for People
  • Biblical Foundation
  • Prayer & Worship
  • Community Impact
