Our History

Rhema Fire Christian Church was founded in 2015 by Apostle Robert Nikoi and Prophetess Comfort Nikoi in Koforidua, Ghana. What began as a small prayer group has grown into a vibrant prophetic ministry impacting lives across multiple countries.

The ministry is built on the pillars of Prayer, Worship, and Prophetic Ministry, with a special emphasis on deliverance and spiritual warfare.

Apostle Robert Nikoi

Founder & Senior Pastor

Apostle Robert Nikoi is a prophetic voice with a mandate to raise the Fire Generation. He operates in apostolic and prophetic anointing with emphasis on deliverance and kingdom advancement.

Prophetess Comfort Nikoi

Co-Founder & Prayer Director

Prophetess Comfort Nikoi leads the prayer and intercession ministry. She has a strong prophetic mantle with emphasis on family restoration and women empowerment.
