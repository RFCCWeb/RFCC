Our Journey of Fire

From humble prayer gatherings to a global fire movement

The Fire Generation Story

Rhema Fire Christian Church (RFCC), known as The Fire Generation, began as a small prayer fellowship in the Eastern Region of Ghana. What started as informal evening prayers among friends has grown into a vibrant ministry marked by passionate worship, fervent prayer, and transformative community impact.

Our Journey Through Time

2014

Formal Beginnings

The prayer fellowship was formally established as Rhema Prayer Klinik in Amanokrom, Akuapem. Meetings were held on open school fields and classrooms, marked by intense intercession and spiritual hunger.

Innovation: Introduced mobile phone conference prayers to overcome weather challenges, laying the foundation for our strong online ministry.

2015

Relocation & Expansion

Founder Robert Ashley Nikoi relocated from Amanokrom to Koforidua. Despite the move, ministry continued through both physical meetings and the growing phone conference prayer system.

The ministry demonstrated remarkable resilience, maintaining fellowship across distances through technological innovation.

2016

Ministerial Ordination

Founder Robert Ashley Nikoi was ordained as a prophet, strengthening the ministry's leadership and spiritual foundation.

He also began serving voluntarily as literary and IT assistant at the Mamfe Prayer Camp, contributing to ministerial publications and church branding.

2017

Rebranding & Growth

Transitioned to Rhema Prayer City Incorporated, operating as a non-denominational prayer and prophetic movement.

Began holding structured prayer meetings at Mountain of Fire and Miracles Church premises in Koforidua and launched extensive evangelistic outreaches across multiple communities.

2017-2019

Retreat Ministry

Organized biannual prayer retreats at Patmos Retreat Center near Larteh Junction:

  • Prayer Trek: Mid-year retreats (June-August)
  • Take Back: December retreats

Participants experienced intensive teaching, prayer, deliverance, prophetic direction, and healing over three-day periods.

2020

Major Crusade & Pandemic Adaptation

Held first major Evangelism and Cross-Over Crusade at Ohemaa Park, Betom-Koforidua on December 30-31.

Successfully transitioned to full online ministry during COVID-19, maintaining weekly services, Bible studies, morning devotions, and deliverance sessions through phone conference calls.

2025

Strategic Rebranding

Began transition to Rhema Fire Christian Church (RFCC), clarifying our identity as a church while preserving our prophetic and prayer-centered heritage.

New Vision: RFCC serves as the church expression, while Rhema Prayer City evolves into a network of retreat centers for prayer, deliverance, and prophetic empowerment.

Present

Current Ministry & Future Vision

Continuing strong online presence with consistent prayer meetings, Bible studies, and worship services.

Actively seeking permanent worship facility in Koforidua while maintaining vibrant community outreach and evangelism.

Planning revival of major soul-winning crusades from 2028 onward.

Founder & Lead Pastor

Prophet Robert Ashley Nikoi

Prophetic Minister, Teacher, Author, Technology Professional

Pastor Robert Ashley Nikoi is a Ghanaian prophetic minister with a strong mandate in prayer, deliverance, and prophetic empowerment. He emerged as the prayer leader during the early gatherings in Amanokrom, where his dedication to intercession and spiritual discipline shaped the foundation of what would become The Fire Generation.

Education & Academics
